Privacy Policy for "Criminal Cooperation"

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stefano Gambarin ("we", "us") handles user data in the video game "Criminal Cooperation".

1. Data Collection

This application does not collect personal information through forms or account registration on our own servers. However, the application requires the use of third-party platform accounts to function.

When playing the game, the software processes the following technical identifiers temporarily in memory:

  • Platform User ID: A numeric identifier used to distinguish players during a session.
  • IP Address: Required to establish Peer-to-Peer (P2P) connections between the host player and the client player.

2. Use of Data

The technical data mentioned above is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To enable multiplayer connectivity (matchmaking and lobby creation).
  • To synchronize game state between the two players (Agent and Hacker).

We do not use this data for analytics, marketing, or tracking purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This game uses Epic Online Services to facilitate network features.

If you choose to sign in, authentication is handled externally by the platform provider. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your login credentials (email, password) or payment information.

The collection and use of data by the platform provider are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain user data.

  • We do not operate a backend database for user profiles.
  • Gameplay data exists only during the active game session and is discarded when the session ends or the application is closed.
  • Game progress (save files) is stored locally on your device.
