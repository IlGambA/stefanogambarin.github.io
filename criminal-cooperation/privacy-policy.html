Privacy Policy for "Criminal Cooperation"

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stefano Gambarin ("we", "us") handles user data in the video game "Criminal Cooperation". We are committed to transparency regarding the limited technical data processed to enable multiplayer features.

1. How Data is Captured

We do not require you to create a proprietary account with us, nor do we use forms to collect personal information manually. Data is captured automatically through the Epic Online Services (EOS) SDK integration when you:

  • Launch the application and authenticate using your existing Epic Games account.
  • Initiate or join a multiplayer session (Lobby).
  • Interact with other players via the in-game networking system.

2. How Data is Used

We process specific technical data solely to provide the game's core functionality. Specifically:

  • Authentication: Your Epic Account ID is used to verify your identity with Epic Games servers, ensuring you are a legitimate user.
  • Matchmaking & Invites: Your Product User ID and Online Presence status are used to allow you to send/receive game invites and find your friends' lobbies.
  • Peer-to-Peer Connectivity: Your IP Address is processed transiently to establish a direct network connection between you and the other player (Agent vs Hacker). This is necessary to synchronize game state (position, actions) in real-time.

3. How Data is Shared

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third-party data brokers. Data sharing is strictly limited to technical necessities:

  • With Epic Games: Authentication data is exchanged directly between your game client and Epic Games, Inc. to facilitate login. This is subject to Epic Games' Privacy Policy.
  • With Other Players (P2P): Because this game uses Peer-to-Peer networking, your IP address may be visible to the specific player you are connected with during a match. This is a fundamental requirement of P2P architecture.

4. How Data is Retained

We do not retain your personal data.

  • We do not maintain a backend database of user profiles, gameplay history, or chat logs.
  • All data processing described above occurs in the computer's Random Access Memory (RAM) or on the Epic Games servers.
  • Once you close the game application or terminate the session, any local temporary data regarding the session is discarded.
  • Save files (game progress) are stored locally on your own hard drive and are not uploaded to us.
