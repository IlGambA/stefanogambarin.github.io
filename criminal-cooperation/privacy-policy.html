Privacy Policy for "Criminal Cooperation"

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stefano Gambarin ("we", "us", or "our") handles data in the video game "Criminal Cooperation".

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. We do not collect, store, or share personal data for marketing purposes.

1. Data Collection and Epic Online Services

To provide multiplayer functionality (specifically matchmaking, lobby management, and peer-to-peer connectivity), this game utilizes Epic Online Services (EOS), provided by Epic Games, Inc.

When you play "Criminal Cooperation", the following data may be processed via EOS:

  • Epic Account ID / Product User ID: To identify you within the game session and allow you to invite friends.
  • IP Address: To establish a direct Peer-to-Peer (P2P) connection between the "Agent" and the "Hacker" players.
  • Gameplay Data: Technical data required to synchronize the game state between players (e.g., character position, interaction status).

We do not store this data on external servers owned by us. All data processing occurs transiently to facilitate the multiplayer session.

2. Third-Party Services

Since the game uses Epic Account Services for authentication, your login credentials and friend list data are handled directly by Epic Games subject to their privacy policy. We do not have access to your password or payment information.

For more information on how Epic Games handles data, please visit the Epic Games Privacy Policy.

3. Data Retention

We do not maintain a database of user profiles. Game progress and settings are saved locally on your device.
